Pizza

Description:

okay now this.... is a special one

?did you know? that pizza has been voted the greatest food of all time EVERY SINGLE YEAR since it was invented?

Ingredients:

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • fresh basil

Steps:

  1. top that dough with some tangy sauce, leaving some room for a crust
  2. drop a few bits of cheese on there (not too much)
  3. throw into the oven at like 800 degrees. cook for 8 mins
  4. in the last minute or so, sprinkle that basil onto there, wow
